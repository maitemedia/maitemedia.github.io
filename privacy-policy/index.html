Privacy Policy

This policy is based on the current behavior of the Maite Media website and is limited to what the site actually does today.

What this site collects

The current site intentionally collects only the contact details and project information a visitor chooses to share through the contact page or direct contact routes.

Contact form fields

  • Name
  • Company / Organization
  • Email address
  • Project Type
  • Location
  • Timeline
  • Project Summary

Direct contact routes

If you email Maite Media directly or open the WhatsApp route, the information you choose to include is handled through those services rather than stored by the site itself.

Current form behavior

The live site form currently prepares a mailto-based inquiry. It opens the visitor’s email app with the submitted details so the visitor can choose whether to send the message.

How information is used

The site is used to support qualified business conversations about audiovisual production services in Paraguay and Mercosur.

Primary purposes

  • Respond to project inquiries
  • Understand production scope, location, and timing
  • Decide whether the brief is a fit for Maite Media’s services
  • Continue communication by email or WhatsApp when requested by the visitor

No non-essential tracking in current site code

The current site code does not implement newsletter signups, user accounts, payment flows, ad retargeting, analytics platforms, marketing pixels, reCAPTCHA, or map embeds.

Third-party services and external routes

Some interactions leave the site and are then governed by the third party the visitor chooses to use.

Email

The inquiry form currently relies on the visitor’s default email application through a mailto: route. After the visitor sends the message, the content is handled by the visitor’s mail provider and Maite Media’s email service.

WhatsApp

Clicking the WhatsApp route opens wa.me / WhatsApp, which is a separate third-party service.

YouTube

The work page links to public YouTube videos and loads YouTube thumbnail images. The site does not embed YouTube players directly in the current build.

Cookies and similar technologies

No first-party analytics cookies, tracking pixels, or non-essential cookie scripts were identified in the current site code. External services you open after leaving the site may use their own cookies under their own policies.

Requests, retention, and contact

If you want to ask about information you shared through this site, use the approved contact route below.

Questions or requests

Email info@maitemedia.com and describe the request clearly. Maite Media can review reasonable privacy-related requests concerning information received through the site’s contact routes.

Retention approach

Inquiry details are kept only as needed to review, answer, and manage legitimate project conversations, unless a longer period is required for an active business relationship or a legal obligation.

Updates

This page should be revised when the site begins using new forms, analytics, embeds, cookies, or other data-handling tools.
