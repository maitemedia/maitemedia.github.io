When the event needs to be seen in real time

Live streaming is the right fit for conferences, hybrid events, institutional announcements, internal broadcasts, and public communication initiatives.

Built around the event model

Some productions require a straightforward live feed. Others require a more structured setup with multiple cameras, on-site coordination, speaker management, and event timing aligned to the stream.

This service is a fit for

  • Conferences and business forums
  • Hybrid events
  • Institutional announcements
  • Internal company broadcasts
  • Stakeholder-facing events

Live production leaves less room for error

Unlike other content formats, a live stream does not benefit from post-production correction.

Why reliability matters

Technical issues, poor planning, weak communication, and unclear responsibilities can affect the event in real time. That is why buyers typically care about one thing above all: confidence in execution.

How Maite Media supports live productions

We help define the technical path, align expectations, support on-site execution, and keep the production organized from setup to live delivery.

Frequently asked questions

Do you support live and hybrid events?

Yes. This page is built specifically for live-only and hybrid formats that need dependable technical coordination.

Can live streaming be combined with event video coverage?

Yes. Many projects combine live delivery with event coverage or post-event assets.

Do you work with institutional and corporate teams?

Yes. The service is designed for corporate, nonprofit, institutional, and agency-led live productions.

How early should we plan a live stream?

As early as possible, especially when timing, venue conditions, or stakeholder coordination will affect the production model.
