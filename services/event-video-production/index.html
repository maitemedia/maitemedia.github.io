Built for events that need more than casual coverage

This service supports conferences, business forums, launches, internal company events, nonprofit gatherings, and institutional programs.

Coverage shaped around the event goal

Event productions may include full-session capture, highlight edits, interview clips, social-ready assets, speaker coverage, audience and environment visuals, and recap content for internal or external communication.

Typical event formats

  • Conferences and summits
  • Business forums and industry events
  • Corporate launches and announcements
  • Institutional gatherings
  • Nonprofit events and public-facing initiatives

Strong event coverage starts before the event begins

Event shoots often involve live timing, limited access windows, multiple stakeholders, and no second take.

Why event production needs planning

Event video production is not simply about showing up with cameras. It requires planning, coordination, and a clear understanding of what content matters most.

Maite Media helps clients define a practical event coverage plan before production starts, so execution stays aligned with the event schedule and communication goals.
