In production, geography affects planning, timing, coordination, crew logic, communication flow, and how much local execution support is needed to keep a project controlled and efficient.

Why this section exists

Not to make a vague regional claim, but to help buyers understand where Maite Media is strongest, how projects are grounded operationally, and how Paraguay connects to broader regional work.
